The Photographer

My name is Danka Dzurišová. I was born on 27th November in 1992. I am from Slovakia but I am living in the South Wales (Great Britain). I remember when I was younger I started took pictures with my old mobile with small screen. That was really fun. When I was 16 years old I took pictures of my friends with mobile. In 2013 I bought my first camera Nikon D3200. I was soo happy. First moments are always the best. I am still using this camera with my second Nikon D 7500 bought on 27 th April in 2021. I love them both. Photography is my freedom and passion. With my photos I want to show you the world I see through lenses . I love taking photos of animals, nature, country. I do portrait photos. I hope you like it my portfolio. Thank you for visiting my website and feel free to answer any questions.
